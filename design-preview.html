Our thesis

Rules of engagement should be expressible, enforceable, and measurable. Sites should have real leverage—not just legal text.

Expressible

Sites can declare nuanced preferences beyond simple allow or disallow. Search indexing vs. AI training? Different rules.

Enforceable

Technical controls at the network edge make violating rules costly. Honest access becomes easier than cheating.

Measurable

Instrumentation tracks compliance, detects violations, and provides evidence for accountability.

From "please don't" to technical enforcement.
